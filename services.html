Brow design

The signature appointment. We start by mapping your brows against your bone structure, then shape with wax and tweezers, and finish with a tint matched to your colouring.

The goal is a shape you can maintain at home between visits, not one that collapses the moment it grows out.

View service menu

Brow lamination

Lamination softly sets your brow hairs in a fuller, brushed-up direction that holds for weeks. It is ideal if your brows are unruly, flat, or sparse in places, and it pairs beautifully with a shape and tint.

Every lamination ends with a nourishing treatment to keep the hairs healthy, and you will get simple aftercare guidance to make it last.
