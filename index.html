Meet your artist

Nicola Reilly

The Brow Fairy is a one-woman studio built on a simple idea: brows and makeup should flatter the person wearing them, not follow a trend. Nicola trained across brow design, lamination, lash treatments and micropigmentation, and keeps building on that foundation, most recently as a 2026 UK Hair & Beauty Awards finalist for Best for Brows, representing Suffolk.

Good to know

Frequently asked questions

What happens at a first appointment?

Every visit starts with a short, honest consultation, so the result suits your features and your routine. There is no obligation and no pressure; it is simply the best way to check we are a good fit.

Do I need a patch test first?

Yes, a patch test is required at least 48 hours before your first tint, lamination or micropigmentation appointment. It is free and takes about 15 minutes. See the pricing page for details.

How do I book, and how quickly will I hear back?

Book directly through the online booking system, or send an enquiry via the contact page with the service you are after and your preferred days. You will typically hear back within a day or two.

What is your cancellation policy?

Please give as much notice as you can if you need to rearrange. Full details on deposits, late cancellations and payments are on the policies page.

How do I look after my results afterwards?

Every treatment comes with clear aftercare guidance on the day. The aftercare hub also has guides for each treatment, healing timelines and general do’s and don’ts.
