Her story

A softer way to do brows

The Brow Fairy is Nicola: brow artist, makeup artist, and firm believer that brows should be shaped to flatter the person wearing them. No harsh blocks, no one-size-fits-all stencils, just careful work that makes the most of what you already have.

Appointments are unhurried and one-on-one, so there is time to talk through what you like, what you have tried before, and what will genuinely work day to day.

Whether you are after a subtle tidy-up, a full redesign, or makeup for the biggest day of your life, you will leave looking like yourself on a very good day.

  • 2026 UK Hair & Beauty Awards finalist, Best for Brows
