Policies

The small print, with a heart. Here is how bookings work.

Late cancellations

I kindly ask for a minimum of 48 hours notice if you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment. This allows me time to offer the appointment to another client who may be waiting.

Cancellations made with less than 48 hours notice will incur a cancellation fee. No-shows will be charged 100% of the appointment cost.

I understand that things happen, and I will always be fair and understanding where I can.

Deposits

A deposit is required to secure your appointment. It will be either a fixed amount or a percentage of your treatment cost, confirmed at the time of booking.

Deposits are non-refundable, but they are deducted from your final balance.

Appointments cannot be booked without a deposit.

Payments

Full payment is required on the day of your appointment unless a payment plan has been agreed.

I accept cash and card payments. Please ensure any payment plans are kept up to date.

please remember...

My time is dedicated to you. When you do not show or cancel last minute, that time goes to waste.

Other clients may miss out on an appointment they need.

Respecting my policies means I can continue to give you the best experience possible.

These policies are here to protect my time, my business and to ensure fairness for all clients. I appreciate your kindness, understanding and support.
